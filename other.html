Museums In NYC

If it's to cold out and you still want to check out some art instead of walking around all over NYC below you have sevral art museums in NYC you could go and visit.

The Musueum of Modern Art or also known as the MoMA is often considered as one of the largest and most influential museums of modern art in the world.The MoMA has so many art exhibits that you could walk in and just look around at all the art work.

The Neue Galerie is a museum of early twentieth century German and Austrian art and design this Galerie has many painting and art exhibits to look around even though it's a small museum.

The Museum of Street Art or also known as the MoSA is a museum with a lot of street art in it made by 20 graffiti artist that came together from the orginal 5pointz since 5pointz was demolished the artist wanted to give 5pointz back to the people by creating the Museum of Street Art which is a building with 21 floors filler with street art.
